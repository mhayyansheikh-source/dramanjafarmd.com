# Medical Practice Website Layout Structure

This document outlines the recommended layout, pages, forms, and UI components for the Dr. Aman Jafar MD website.

## 1. Core Pages (Navigation Structure)
*   **Home:** The landing page. Features a strong hero section, services overview, trust indicators (testimonials), and a clear "Book Appointment" call to action.
*   **About Us / Meet the Doctor:** Detailed biography of Dr. Aman Jafar, credentials, philosophy of care, and information about the clinic staff.
*   **Services / Treatments:** A comprehensive list of medical services offered (e.g., Internal Medicine, Preventative Care, Chronic Disease Management).
*   **Patient Resources:** A hub for patients containing insurance information, billing details, FAQs, and a portal login link.
*   **Contact Us:** Clinic address, Google Maps embed, phone number, fax, business hours, and an embedded contact form.

## 2. Essential Forms
*   **Multi-Step Appointment Booking Form:** A user-friendly form to capture patient details, preferred dates, and reason for visit.
*   **New Patient Intake Form:** A secure form (or downloadable PDF) for medical history, demographics, and consent.
*   **General Inquiry Contact Form:** A simple form on the Contact page for general questions.
*   **Prescription Refill Request:** A streamlined form for existing patients to request medication refills.

## 3. Blog / Content Strategy
*   **Health & Wellness Tips:** Seasonal advice (e.g., "Tips for Flu Season", "Managing Allergies").
*   **Practice Updates:** Announcements about new services, updated hours, or new staff members.
*   **Condition Spotlights:** Informational articles on common conditions treated at the clinic (e.g., "Understanding Hypertension", "Diabetes Management Basics").

## 4. Global Layout Components
*   **Top Bar (Urgency/Fomo):** A thin banner at the very top for announcements like "Now Accepting New Patients!" or "Flu Shots Available."
*   **Sticky Header:** Logo on the left, clean navigation links in the center, and a prominent "Patient Portal" or "Book Now" button on the right.
*   **Footer:**
    *   *Column 1:* Clinic logo and short "about" blurb.
    *   *Column 2:* Quick links (Home, About, Services, Blog).
    *   *Column 3:* Contact info (Address, Phone, Email).
    *   *Column 4:* Legal links (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, HIPAA Compliance Notice).

## 5. Reusable UI Sections (Components)
*   **Testimonials/Reviews Slider:** Showcasing positive patient feedback to build trust.
*   **Service Categories Grid:** Visual cards linking to different specialities or treatments.
*   **Call to Action (CTA) Banners:** Sections interspersed throughout the site prompting users to schedule a visit.
